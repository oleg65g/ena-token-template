INTRODUCING THE ENA TOKEN THE CURRENCY FOR THE EARNATHON ECOSYSTEM

Earnathon is a platform facilitating the global exposure and adoption of blockchain and cryptocurrency through a learn and earn educational model. Learners earn ENA, the native token of the Earnathon platform.

USECASES
With blockchain technology and cryptocurrency offering defining possibilities and benefits to individuals, institutions and government, it becomes imperative to facilitate the learning of this novel technology.
Community Reward

Learners and Earnathon Participants are rewarded with the ENA for learning and participating in the platform growth activities.

Staking

Staking ENA allows Earnathon participants and learners to contribute to the platform growth, and also earn more ENA.

Yield Farming

The ENA token can be farmed across multiple platforms to earn amazing yields.

Product launchpad

ENA will be the vehicle through which products can be launched through the Earnathon platform.
